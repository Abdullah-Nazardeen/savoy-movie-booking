About Savoy

Welcome to Savoy, the premier movie theater in Sri Lanka. We are dedicated to providing an exceptional movie-going experience with state-of-the-art facilities and a wide range of services.

History

Established in 1969, Savoy has been a cornerstone of entertainment in Sri Lanka. Over the years, we have evolved with the times, continuously upgrading our facilities and services to offer the best cinematic experience.

Mission and Vision

Mission: To provide an unparalleled cinematic experience that brings stories to life and creates lasting memories for our patrons.

Vision: To be the leading movie theater in Sri Lanka, known for excellence in service, comfort, and technology.

Goals

At Savoy, our goals are to:

  • Continuously enhance our facilities and services.
  • Offer a diverse range of movies to cater to all audiences.
  • Provide exceptional customer service.
  • Engage with the community through various events and initiatives.
